About Us

SHADAB HEART & LUNG CARE CENTER is a dedicated cardiology and pulmonology clinic located in Gopalganj, Bihar. Led by experienced specialists, we offer comprehensive care for heart and lung conditions with services like OPD consultation, ECG, Echocardiography, TMT (Treadmill Test), Holter Monitoring and PFT. Our mission is to provide accurate diagnosis, effective treatment, and compassionate care in a modern and patient-friendly environment. We are committed to improving the health and well-being of our community with personalized attention and the latest medical technologies. Visit us for expert cardiac and respiratory care.
